Maple Love

A GMS-like v83 MapleStory private server which aims to create a challenging yet fun environment with our low rates.

No pay-to-win or vote-to-win, so you'll feel assured knowing that your hard work pays off.

SERVER INFORMATION

MS Version: 83

Experience Rate: 4x

Meso Rate: 2x

Drop Rate: Custom

Quest Rate: 5x

FEATURES

Rate

4x Exp is the sweet spot when it comes to old-school MapleStory. Our drop rate is custom - meaning each item is adjusted to a desirable rate.

Widgets

We've designed a range of tools in the form of Widgets, which are embedded within the client. They can be accessed from the UI in-game.

Fair Play Loot System

Items dropped by bosses within an expedition are retained in a Loot Pool which is evenly distributed to all eligible participating members.

Classes

Choose from Adventurers, Knights of Cygnus or Aran. All classes have their level limit increased to 300.

Exp Distribution

Our exp distibution is tailored to minimize leeching, encourage grinding, as well as party play. The ratio is 100:20 with a party of 2.

Monster Book

Explore the Maple World and collect the Monster Cards! Bonus stats will be applied to your Crusader Codex for each completed set and tier.

Custom Skills

Experience a new feel for the game with real custom-made skills included with our 4th job Knights of Cygnus.

Party Play Bonus

Our Party Play Bonus system allows attackers that get together in a party, to gain 30% bonus exp for themself. The party must contain at least 2 attackers on the same map.

Imported Content

Along with the classic regions that GMS has to offer, we have imported Ulu City, Shaolin Temple, Shanghai, Ninja Castle, Neo City : Year 2227, Henesys Ruins, Dark Ereve, Knight Stronghold.

Weekly Challenges

Are you up for a challenge? Be rewarded with generous exp for completing up to 7 challenges each week. Players get to pick and choose from a pool of challenges designed for their level bracket.

No HP Washing

Players do not need to worry about HP washing here - we've disabled it. Strengthening Scrolls are obtainable from certain expeditions and can be consumed to provide enough stats to progress.

Automated Events

Take a break from grinding and earn prizes by participating in our Automated Events at the Event Gala, access via the Free Market. Events are hosted every 6 hours starting from midnight.

Maker

Crafting strengthening gems/crystals just got a whole lot more exciting! Players have a chance to gain a gem/crystal of higher tier when crafting their preferred gem/crystal. Furthermore, we've designed Maker so that all items crafted can never possess below-average stats.

Skill Changes

Old-school MS without skill changes can be daunting. We have applied numerous skill changes to provide a more balanced environment. Every class also have a range of skills increased to level 40 to account for the increased level limit.

Patcher

Our Patcher was developed to only make the necessary edits to your game files - this means patches are generally small in size, making the patching process seamless.

Expeditions

Participate in expeditions twice a day to obtain Inkwell Coins which can be traded in for items to assist in progression. Our Expedition Widget mimics the official UI and allows seamless party management.

Equality

We support same-sex marriage and have removed gender restrictions on all items.

Feedback

We respect our community at MS Love and value your feedback. We are committed to accepting suggestions and quashing bugs. Visit our discord and speak to us.
